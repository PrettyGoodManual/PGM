A Guide to PGP

We believe that private communication via email should be accessible to everyone. Basic email, however, is public and unprotected. That means that many parties on the internet can read your every word. PGP ensures that only you and your intended communication partners can read your messages. This website aims at providing a simple and comprehensible guide to email encryption with PGP.
